Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 May 2026

Remindit is a life admin reminder app. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Reminder data (stored locally on your device)

The reminders you create are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. This data never leaves your device.

Anonymous usage analytics (Firebase Analytics)

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used in aggregate. No personally identifiable information is included.

Anonymous authentication (Firebase Auth)

The app uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication. No account or personal information is required.

Usage statistics (Firestore)

We store anonymous per-user statistics: reminder counts per category, preset types in use, last session date, and app version — linked to your anonymous Firebase Auth ID only.

Feedback submissions (Formspree)

If you submit feedback through the in-app form, your message and email are sent via Formspree. This is optional and only occurs when you actively submit.

Crash reports (Sentry)

Anonymous crash data (device type, OS version, app version, stack trace) is collected via Sentry. No reminder content or personal information is included.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the app and deliver local notifications
  • To understand aggregate usage and improve the app
  • To diagnose and fix crashes
  • To respond to feedback you choose to send

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Formspree — formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy
  • Sentry — sentry.io/privacy

4. Data Retention

  • Local reminder data: on your device until you delete the app.
  • Firebase analytics: up to 14 months per Google standard policy.
  • Firestore user stats: anonymous, indefinite. Contact us via in-app feedback to request deletion.
  • Feedback: retained until no longer needed to respond.
  • Crash reports: 90 days in Sentry.

5. Your Rights

  • Delete local data by uninstalling the app
  • Request Firestore deletion via the in-app feedback form
  • Opt out of analytics: iOS Settings > Privacy > Analytics; Android Settings > Google > Ads

If you are in the UK or EEA, you have rights under UK GDPR / GDPR including access, rectification, and erasure.

6. Children's Privacy

Remindit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will reflect any changes.

8. Contact

Questions or deletion requests can be submitted using the feedback form in the app (Settings -> Send Feedback).
